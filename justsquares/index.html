justsquares

justsquares(as of now I can't think of a better name) is a Terraria-like game
using the olc::PixelGameEngine. I started the development on April 2020, so
there is not much to see. Tell me what you think at segfaultycode@gmail.com, any feedback would be greatly appreciated.


As most of the main engine is finished, the game is now in the "alpha" stage, and I would love to implement any ideas you have.

Follow me on Twitter :)
